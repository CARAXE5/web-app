Booking Policies

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Please give us at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a cancellation fee.

Arrival Time

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows you to check in, relax, and prepare for your service. If you arrive late, your service may need to be adjusted or rescheduled.

Payment Methods

We accept cash, all major credit cards, mobile money transfers (MTN Money, Airtel Money), and bank transfers. Payment is due at the time of service.
