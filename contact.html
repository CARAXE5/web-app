Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome when available, we highly recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred barber, date, and time.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance for weekdays and 5-7 days for weekends to secure your preferred time slot.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, mobile money transfers (MTN Money, Airtel Money), and bank transfers.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. They make a perfect gift for any occasion and can be purchased in-store or by phone.
